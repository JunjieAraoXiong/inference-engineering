# Notes: Preface

## One-Sentence Thesis

Inference engineering is becoming a core AI engineering discipline because open and custom models make latency, reliability, and cost direct product advantages.

## Core Ideas

- Inference is now a strategic layer, not just "call an API."
- Open models change the economics: teams can tune, deploy, and operate their own intelligence.
- The field rewards engineers who can connect product quality, model behavior, GPU performance, and production systems.
- The book is a map of the stack rather than a narrow recipe book.

## Why This Matters Personally

The opportunity is not just knowing terms like vLLM or quantization. The useful skill is being able to look at an AI product and decide:

- what quality bar the model must clear,
- what latency the user actually feels,
- what the dominant cost driver is,
- what part of the stack is the current bottleneck,
- and which optimization is worth the engineering complexity.

## Things To Revisit

- Track examples of products where inference quality, speed, or cost changes the product category.
- Keep a running list of open models that are good enough for specific workloads.
